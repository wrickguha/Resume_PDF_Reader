About Us

SmartSummarizer is a web application designed to streamline document interaction and analysis, with a focus on PDF files. This application provides a suite of tools to help users get the most out of their documents. You can chat with your PDFs, extract key information and compare multiple documents. This makes it ideal for tasks like resume screening, where recruiters can quickly extract candidate data and compare qualifications, or for anyone needing to quickly understand the content of complex PDF documents. SmartSummarizer leverages AI to enhance efficiency and understanding.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize document interaction by providing intelligent tools that simplify information access and analysis, empowering users to make data-driven decisions with ease.

What We Offer

Chat with PDF: Upload your PDF documents and engage in a conversational manner. Ask specific questions about the content, and our intelligent system will provide accurate and relevant answers, making it easier to extract information from lengthy or complex documents.

Extract Resume Info: Simplify the resume screening process by automatically extracting essential information from resumes. Get structured data on candidate qualifications, experience, and skills, saving valuable time and reducing manual effort.

Compare Resumes: Make informed hiring decisions by comparing multiple resumes simultaneously. Identify key differences and similarities between candidates, allowing you to quickly pinpoint the most qualified individuals for the job.
